# Site of the Day 2026 — Curated References

A non-exhaustive list of recent Awwwards Site of the Day winners (Dec 2025 — May 2026) with notes on what makes each distinctive. Use as reference points when describing aesthetic direction, not as templates.

## How to use this list

Each entry has:
- **Site name and URL pattern** (no clickable links — search by name).
- **Archetype lean** — which archetype from `skills/marketing-design/archetypes/` it represents.
- **Distinctive signals** — what makes it stand out.
- **Tech stack indicators** — if observable.
- **What to borrow / what to avoid copying** — calibration notes.

## WebGL / Three.js dominant

### Floema
- **Archetype:** Kinetic Experimental, with editorial undercurrent.
- **Signals:** Custom WebGL transitions between sections, dragged image sliders with physics, ABC Diatype Mono for UI text, near-monochrome palette with one warm accent.
- **Tech:** Three.js, GSAP, Lenis, Next.js.
- **Borrow:** Page transition choreography, the rhythm of letting motion breathe.
- **Avoid copying:** The specific physics-based image slider — it's their signature.

### XOX
- **Archetype:** Kinetic Experimental.
- **Signals:** Hero shader with mouse-responsive distortion, oversized typography in motion, brutalist color blocks intercut with refined type.
- **Tech:** Three.js, GLSL shaders, GSAP.
- **Borrow:** The contrast between brutalist color and refined type.
- **Avoid:** Trying to match the shader complexity without a shader-capable engineer on team.

### T11 / The Eleven
- **Archetype:** Kinetic Experimental, agency.
- **Signals:** Full-page WebGL fluid sim background, magnetic cursor, custom case study transitions.
- **Tech:** React Three Fiber, GSAP, Lenis.
- **Borrow:** Case study transition pattern (full-screen takeover, slow reveal).
- **Avoid:** Fluid sim background unless the brand earns it.

### Detroit Paris (agency)
- **Archetype:** Kinetic Experimental.
- **Signals:** Marquee text bands, scroll-pinned sections, oversized type with WebGL displacement on hover.
- **Borrow:** Marquee + scroll-pin combo for agency homepages.

### Ruinart (heritage / luxury)
- **Archetype:** Editorial Luxury with kinetic touches.
- **Signals:** Heritage champagne house using WebGL bottle visualizations, slow typography reveals, cream/gold palette.
- **Borrow:** How a heritage brand integrates modern tech without losing heritage register.

### Oryzo (AI product)
- **Archetype:** Kinetic Experimental + Soft Structuralism.
- **Signals:** Three.js abstract forms representing AI computation, dark surface with bright single accent.
- **Borrow:** Visualizing the invisible (AI) with abstract 3D.

## Editorial Luxury / Magazine

### Anthropic (anthropic.com)
- **Archetype:** Editorial Luxury / Minimalist Editorial.
- **Signals:** Cream surface, near-black warm ink, oversized serif headings (Tiempos), monospace accents, photographic illustrations, minimal motion.
- **Tech:** Next.js, custom CSS.
- **Borrow:** The restraint, the photographic editorial direction.
- **Avoid:** Copying the exact typeface combination (Tiempos + Styrene).

### Cal.com (caldotcom 2025 redesign)
- **Archetype:** Soft Structuralism with editorial accent.
- **Signals:** Light surface, single confident accent (their signature green-cyan), Mono accent type, generous whitespace.
- **Tech:** Next.js, Tailwind, Framer Motion.
- **Borrow:** How a SaaS site can read editorial without sacrificing CTA prominence.

### Resend (resend.com)
- **Archetype:** Minimalist Editorial.
- **Signals:** Black surface, single gradient signature element, refined developer-tool aesthetic, Geist typography.
- **Borrow:** Owning a single signature visual element (their gradient).

### Linear (linear.app)
- **Archetype:** Soft Structuralism with kinetic accents.
- **Signals:** The original "Linear effect" — dark surface, bento grids, scroll-triggered reveals, Inter Display (custom variant).
- **Note:** Still influential, but the look is now widely copied. Use as influence, not template.

### Vercel (vercel.com)
- **Archetype:** Kinetic Experimental + Soft Structuralism.
- **Signals:** WebGL globe hero, Geist typography (their own), heavy bento grids, dark surface with gradient accents.
- **Borrow:** How a tech company can ship distinctive every quarter.

## Patterns to take from this list

**If you can only borrow one pattern from each tier:**
- **From Editorial Luxury:** the pacing. Things take longer. Whitespace is more generous.
- **From Kinetic Experimental:** the case study transition (full-screen reveals).
- **From Soft Structuralism:** restraint on chroma + one confident accent.
- **From Minimalist Editorial:** transparent pricing or transparent process.
- **From Maximalist:** rare — most projects shouldn't reach for this.
- **From Ethereal Glass:** subtle backdrop-blur in component layers (not background).

## When to actually look at these

When a client says "I want it to look like [generic competitor]" — pull 3-5 references from this list in the appropriate archetype. Discussing concrete examples shifts the brief from vague to specific within one meeting.

Do not show this list to clients unmediated. Curate to 3-5 per project conversation.
